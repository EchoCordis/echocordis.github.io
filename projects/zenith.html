ZENITH

A cyberpunk themed 2D platformer game, built using the Unity engine.

Role

Context

Date
UI Designer / Level Designer University Project 2020

OVERVIEW
Zenith was developed during a group assignment that was structured similarily to a game jam, which required the project to incorporate two themes: "Let's try this again" & "Change your perspective".

This project was developed in partnership with Thalia Johnson and Peter Deghaim.

My preferred future career would be a career as a game developer. It wouldn't matter if I worked for a major gaming company or as an independent game developer. However, I wouldn't mind working for a company like Valve or Nintendo, as they have made some of my favourite games. Personally, I want to develop a game inspired by Kingdom Hearts and the game should have a fast-paced combat system. It would be pretty difficult for me to make design and develop a game all by myself, as I'm not a good artist or a musician because art and music are extremely important for a good game. I could always just make a text-based adventure game, but that would be pretty boring.
