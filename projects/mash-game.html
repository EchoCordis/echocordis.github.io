M*A*S*H* Recreation

A recreation of the Atari 2600 game, M*A*S*H* (1983), built using the Unity engine.

Role

Context

Date
Creator University Project 2021

OVERVIEW
The M*A*S*H* recreation game was developed during an university assignment where I had to recreate the Atari 2600 game, M*A*S*H* (1986). During development, I changed the theme of the game from being based on the show M*A*S*H* to being a game about rescuing farm animals in an UFO. Additional features were also added to the game, such as difficulty settings and randomly spawning obstacles and collectables.
