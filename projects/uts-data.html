UTS DATA VISUALISER

A visualisation of the impact of COVID-19 on the on-campus attendance of UTS students and staff.

Role

Context

Date
Programmer / UI Designer University Project 2020

OVERVIEW
The UTS data visualiser was developed during a group assignment focused on visualising data from the University of Technology Sydney's EIF Research Data Interface . The EIF research data used was collected from sensors placed around UTS Building 11, which detected the number of students and staff entering the building. The sample data used was collected during the period between 1/1/2019 to 10/09/2020.

This project was developed in partnership with Abderraouf Abbou, Charlie Phong and Yuhao Song.
